About Us

We are a Christ-centered community devoted to strengthening marriages by keeping Jesus as the true cornerstone of every relationship and home. Our heart is to create a safe, welcoming, and faith-filled space where couples can come together to grow spiritually, experience healing, receive encouragement, and build deeper, stronger connections with one another through the guidance of God’s Word.

Marriage is a beautiful covenant designed by God, and we believe that when husbands and wives seek Him together, their relationship is renewed with love, grace, unity, and purpose. In every season of life — whether joyful or challenging — we desire to walk alongside couples with compassion, prayer, affirmation, and unwavering support. Through fellowship and shared faith, we remind one another that no marriage is meant to journey alone.

Our gatherings are centered around heartfelt prayer, biblical teaching, meaningful conversations, worship, and authentic fellowship that help couples grow not only as partners, but also as disciples of Christ. We encourage families to build homes rooted in faith, communication, forgiveness, and Christlike love so they may reflect God’s presence in their everyday lives.

We believe God restores what is broken, strengthens what is weak, and continually grows love where hearts remain surrendered to Him. As a community, we celebrate victories together, stand together through difficulties, and encourage one another to pursue marriages that honor God and become a testimony of His faithfulness.

Our mission is to nurture Christ-centered marriages that flourish in faith, healing, unity, and love — raising stronger families and building a community firmly rooted in God’s grace and truth.



Our Mission

To cultivate Christ-centered marriages by creating a faith-filled community where couples are strengthened through prayer, encouraged through authentic fellowship, empowered by God’s Word, and equipped to build marriages anchored in grace, faith, love, and spiritual growth.

Our Vision

To see generations of families transformed through Christ-centered marriages, raising a thriving community of couples whose homes reflect peace, healing, unity, and unwavering faith — shining with Christ’s love, restoring hope, and leaving a lasting legacy for future generations.

With Gratitude

"Honoring those whose lives reflect Christ’s love and faithfulness."


Honoring A Godly Legacy

This fellowship was born through God’s grace and inspired by the beautiful lives and Christ-centered example of our beloved Uncle and Aunty Mr. Victor and Mrs. Selvi Victor.

Their marriage has been a reflection of unwavering faith, sacrificial love, humility, prayer, and steadfast commitment to God and family. Through every season, they have shown what it truly means to build a home upon Christ, our eternal Cornerstone.

Their encouragement, wisdom, prayers, and quiet acts of love have touched many lives and became one of the foundations of this fellowship. What began as a simple desire to strengthen couples was deeply shaped by the godly legacy they continue to carry with grace and faithfulness.

We honor them with grateful hearts and thank God for the example they continue to be — a testimony of love rooted in Christ and a blessing to generations of families.

“One generation shall commend Your works to another.” — Psalm 145:4

Faithful Servant Hearts

We also lovingly honor and thank God for Mr. Suthan David and Mrs. Jemima Suthan, whose sincere love for couples and faithful servant hearts continue to strengthen this fellowship in countless ways.

Through their prayers, encouragement, hospitality, compassion, and willingness to serve without expecting anything in return, they have created a space where couples feel welcomed, valued, encouraged, healed, and spiritually refreshed.

Their lives beautifully reflect the heart of Christ — serving with humility, loving with sincerity, and faithfully walking alongside families through every season of life. More than organizing gatherings, they have helped nurture a community rooted in prayer, fellowship, healing, affirmation, and growing together in God’s love.

We remain deeply grateful for their sacrifice, dedication, and unwavering support toward this ministry and the many families it continues to impact.

“Whoever refreshes others will be refreshed.” — Proverbs 11:25
